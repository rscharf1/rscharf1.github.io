# Regavi

Regavi is a tech startup at Washington University in St. Louis. Regavi is an app that creats "Smart Contacts" and makes connecting with others on several forms of social and professional media very easy. The app works by compiling the social and professional media that you choose to share in a single QR code. Simply show the QR code to people who you would like to connect with and allow them to scan it. Regardless of whether they have Regavi or not, they will instantly have the information you choose to share.
